The Fall Necessities

Soup Spices The Paring Knife Fall Veggies
There is no better time for soups and stews than the season of fall! That means its time to get your kitchen stocked with all the most important spices for soups. To spice up any soup, the essentials include parsley, garlic powder, thyme, rosemary, sage and we can't forget cumin for our chilis! When it comes to fall cooking, you are going to be a lot of chopping and throwing ingredients into soups and stews. That means you need a reliable paring knife! These trusty knives is an all-purpose knife, but it is especially good for peeling and chopping up potatoes, carrots and squash! That sounds like a stew to me! You can't chop up anything for your fall soups and stews if you don't have the right veggies to chop up. The key fall veggies include all the gourds and squashes (think pumpkin, butternut squash, spaghetti squash) as well as carrots and potatoes to make up some hearty soups and stews!
