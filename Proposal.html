Around the World in 80 Dishes

This is a website of recipes from countries and cultures all around the world. The website is different from other cooking websites because it does not focus on one type of food. It includes a wide variety of types so that it can be a one stop website to diversify daily meals! It will not only give the recipes but it will also include helpful hints and tricks for cooking meals from each culture that is included on the website. The color theme of my website will be reds, oranges and yellows because those are the colors that evoke hunger.

Website Objective

The objective of this website is to encourage viewers to diversify their cooking beyond their own culture and recipes they grew up knowing. This is important because appreciation for food is something I truly believe brings people together. By having a single website to go to in order to expand one's food horizons, I think it will help people appreciate cultures they may never have even had the chance to interact with in person!

Target Audience

The target audience for this website is for Millenials and younger GenXers who are looking to eat healthier and diversify their palate. I chose this demographic because they are the group leading the food trends right now and looking for ways to change up their eating. This is a timely topic since we are in a pandemic and one thing people in this demographic have been doing to pass the time is cooking and baking!

Website Content

The content of this website will mostly consist of pages dedicated to different types of recipes. There will also be a homepage, sitemap and contact page to send in recipe ideas. There will be one page devoted to essential spices, ingredients and equiptment needed when cooking food from a specific culture. Recipes that are vegan will be marked with a vegan symbol. The recipes will be categorized by country/culture within each webpage. Each recipe will be a picture accompanied with a description and a link to an outside webpage that has the recipe.

Page Breakdown:
  • Vegetarian Dishes
    • This page will have vegetarian recipes from different cultures.
  • Non-Vegetarian Dishes
    • This page will have non-vegetarian recipes from different cultures.
  • Soups and Pastas
    • This page will have pastas and soups from different cultures.
  • Kitchen Essentials
    • This page will consist of a drop down format that will have information on the essential ingredients/spices and equiptment needed when cooking food from a specific culture.
